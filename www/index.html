February 2012

What will smuggl.it do?

We bring travellers in contact with people with requests for special needs from far away.

For instance: someone in Hong Kong very much wants a type of marmalade that can only be bought in a tiny shop in Madrid. Someone else from Hong Kong may be sitting in a cafe just around the corner of that tiny shop in Madrid.

A rare occasion you think? We're going to prove that this happens all the time an we'll bring these people together.

When can I smuggl.it?
